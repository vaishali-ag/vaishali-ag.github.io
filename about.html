About Us

A Legacy of Authentic South Indian Flavors

Established in 1980 by Dr. Radhakrishnan Salai, Kaaraikudi Restaurant has proudly served authentic South Indian cuisine for over four decades. We honor the rich culinary heritage of Chettinad, offering time-honored recipes prepared in a traditional style .

At Kaaraikudi Indian Multi-Cuisine, we continue this legacy with a fresh, modern twist. Located in the heart of Westmont, our restaurant—formerly known as Shri Bistro now operates under new ownership, blending classic flavors with contemporary innovations.

Whether you're a lifelong lover of South Indian cuisine

or exploring it for the first time, Kaaraikudi Indian Multi-Cuisine invites you to enjoy a culinary journey filled with vibrant flavors and warm hospitality. Join us and savor the true essence of Chettinad cuisine!
