About Us

Kaaraikudi Restaurant established in the year 1980 on Dr.Radhakrishnan Salai proudly boasts serving authentic South Indian food in traditional way ever since. At Kaaraikudi Indian Multi-Cusine, we celebrate tradition with a fresh twist. Located in the heart of Westmont, we carry forward a legacy of over 40 years of fine Indian cuisine, formerly known as Shri Bistro. Under new ownership, our reimagined restaurant blends cherished recipes with bold, modern flavors.
Our dishes are crafted with generous use of pepper, cinnamon, bay leaves, nutmeg, green and red chilies, creating a unique taste experience. We offer a diverse menu of vegetarian and non-vegetarian specialties, including:
Non-Vegetarian Favorites: Chicken 65 (with bone), Chicken Pepper Roast, Vanjaram Fish Fry, Mutton Kola Urundai, Egg Masala, and weekend specials like Mutton Thalakari and Mutton Liver Fry. Vegetarian Delights: Vazhaipoo Vadai, Baby Corn Varuval, Chilli Mushroom, Pakoda Kuzhambu, and Vada Curry.
Our meals are traditionally paired with rice and rice-based accompaniments like dosas, appams, idiyappams, and idlis. Indulge in our signature Kaaraikudi Chettinad meals, featuring rice, sambar, fish gravy, chicken gravy, appalam, keerai, kootu, curry leaf powder with gingelly oil, sweet, and banana—offering an unforgettable culinary journey.
Whether you’re a long-time lover of Indian cuisine or exploring it for the first time, Karakuti Indian Multi-Cuisine is the perfect place to indulge in vibrant flavors and exceptional hospitality.
