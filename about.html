About Us


Kaaraikudi Restaurant established in the year 1980 on Dr.Radhakrishnan Salai proudly boasts serving authentic chettinad food in traditional way ever since. A southern atmosphere has been created inside the restaurant with artifacts from that region, having a seating capacity of 80 covers, which are neatly arranged in a 2000sft of pleasing ambiance. Chettinad cuisine is characterized by liberal use of spices having generous amount of pepper, cinnamon, bay leaves, nutmeg, green & red chillies etc; considered to be one of the oiliest & aromatic cuisine in India. The cuisine offers a variety of Vegetarian & non – vegetarian dishes which are spicy & unique in taste. Some of the non – vegetarian dishes include special chettinad chicken 65 ( with bone ), chicken pepper roast, Vanjaram fish fry, mutton kola uraundai, egg masala etc; Weekend specialities like Mutton Thalakari, Mutton Liver fry & much more to discover from the list of typical Chettinad preparations. Some of the popular vegetarian dishes include vazhaipoo vadai, Baby corn Varuval, chilli mushroom, pakoda kuzhambu,vada curry. Most of the dishes are eaten with rice & rice based accompaniments such as dosas, appam, idiyappam & idili. You can indulge in Kaaraikudi Chettinad meals served with rice, sambar, fish gravy,chicken gravy, appalam, keerai, kootu, curry leaf powder, gingely oil, sweet & banana etc; that takes your palate on a guided creative journey to enjoy your meal. The traditional ambience alongside the melodious old songs from the famous tamil movies will take you to the period. Come, dine in for the best chettinad experience you have ever had.
